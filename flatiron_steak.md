Flatiron Steak Pot Roast
=======================
Made up recipe.

Ingredients
================
* 1 Flatiron steak 
* 1 medium onion, chopped
* 6 cloves garlic
* 1 cup Italian dressing
* 0.25 cup Red Wine Vinegar
* 0.25 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
* 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
* 3-4 carrots, chopped
* 2 pinches of cayenne pepper
* 1 teaspoon Thyme
* 1 teaspoon Herbes de provence
* 2 teaspoons salt
* 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
* Black pepper to taste

Directions
================
* Marinate steak, onion, garlic, italian dressing, and vinegar, covered in a crock pot
in the fridge for 4-5 hours
* Remove from fridge 
* Add carrots and seasoning
* Cook on low heat for 3 hours
* Add frozen vegetables, increase to high heat for 2 hours
* Set heat to "warming" until ready to eat.
